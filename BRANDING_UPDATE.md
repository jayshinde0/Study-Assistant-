## Why MindForge?

The name "MindForge" was chosen because:
- **Mind** - Represents learning, intelligence, and cognitive development
- **Forge** - Suggests building, crafting, and mastering skills
- Together - Implies the process of building and strengthening knowledge through practice

The tagline "Master Your Learning" emphasizes:
- Personalized, adaptive learning paths
- Mastery-based progression
- Student empowerment and control
